🏰 Epic Civilization Game 🏹

Your civilization, discoveries, army, and heroes are in your hands!

How to Play:

  • Start with your civilization and manage your people and resources.
  • Discover technologies and sciences to enhance your army and population.
  • Recruit citizens and form your army according to their traits.
  • Heroes and leaders provide tactical strength and special abilities.
  • The traits of your people and heroes gradually change your civilization identity (Barbaric, Civilized, Mixed).
  • Explore cities and buildings, and invent new strategies based on resources and discoveries.
  • Every decision opens new possibilities and changes the course of the game.
  • Death of the King/main character = game over.

💡 This is a demo version to familiarize you with the game system.
